Becky's favorite movies

Top 3 of all time!

Despicable Me 2

I really love this movie! remains a classic in my watchlist, it stands out for me because of its charisma, also, it has Mexico references and a truly heartwarming love story between our already known main protagonist Gru, and his job teammate, Lucy.

Elemental

Despise being kinda unpopular for a Disney creation, I really like this movie. There are lots of interesting character designs, the development between our main protagonists seems very realistic and cute to me, kind of a forbidden love, with parents disapproval, and on the top of that, they're not supposed to be together due their physical composition.

Belle

I have never been a fan of 3D anime, but Belle made me realize that not all 3D anime productions have to be cringy. This movie is a whole artwork in all senses, music, graphics, animation, design-wise even!
